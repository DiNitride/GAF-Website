GAF Arma Squad

GAF runs a casual mil-sim operations squad, enganging in missions and general Arma shenanigans, with an eventual focus on Zeus gameplay. We're currently getting set up and trying to grow, but if you're interested in big scale, casual mil-sim ops, then we'd love to have you aboard. Just join the Discord server and start whining until someone comes to join you.

Our play style is semi-casual mil-sim. In typical GAF fashion, almost nothing will probably go to plan, everyone will die and someone will massacre half the friendlies. However, hopefully this will be highly entertaining and enjoyable. We're not a dedicated squad with ranks or structures, we will fill roles on the night to whatever roles are needed for the mission, generally without too much foreplanning. That said, we will still operate with Task Force Radio installed on a Teamspeak server and follow a chain of command for the mission. How we play will largely evolve as we grow a squad playerbase, so feel free to join and push suggestions into how we can make it better.
